About Us

Mission

HHCC creates a safe environment by emphasizing precautions, safety, and welfare of our patients. We build a work environment where each person is valued, respected, and has equal rights.

Vision

To inspire better health in the community and beyond by providing patient-centered care with excellence in quality, service, and access.

History

Healing Hands Care Clinic is a public health center that provides quality services and excellent medical care to our patients. HHCC is located in the Banue Corazel District. HHCC was founded on 4th July 2000 as a donation of the U.S. Embassy. Healing Hands was founded with only 1 doctor and 5 nurses. As the time went by and our clinic has been developing, we now consist of 5 well-organized doctors and 20 nurses; the finest. HHCC wants to continue providing the best services to our patients.
