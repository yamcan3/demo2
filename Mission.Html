International Missions

Guatemala Mission

HHCC began working in Guatemala in September 2022 to provide crucial care in time of chaos. We have been providing medical treatments, medical supplies and equipment, serving more than 12,000 with over 900 volunteers and staff.

Cuba Mission

HHCC began its partnering with Cuba in 2024. We have worked with 300 volunteers to serve more than 3,500 patients. HHCC provided medical treatments, medical supplies and equipment. HHCC continues to help Cuba to increase personal hygiene by educating residents about the importance of proper hand washing techniques.
